Game Design

Here is a list of the various game development projects I've contributed to! I focus mostly on UI art and game-related assets, such as characters, sprites, and objects, but I also dabble in some game scripting.

I've split up the list into major contributions (where I was an integral member of the development process) and minor contributions (where I worked on only a few assets). Click on the project title to visit the game's sitepage and see a gameplay demo!

Image courtesy of Wallpaper Cave.
